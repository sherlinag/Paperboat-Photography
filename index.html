Paperboat is a wedding photography studio based in Tamil Nadu. We specialize in creative and artistic Tamil Nadu wedding photography with a natural relaxed style. Since 2025, couples have chosen Paperboat wedding studio for our breathtaking photography, relentless professionalism, and personalized service. Our style is romantic, timeless, and authentic.
